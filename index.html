RAnA

Autonomous agent rental marketplace for workflow automation.

A marketplace where businesses rent autonomous AI agents to handle repetitive workflows and tasks without human oversight. Businesses browse, deploy, and manage AI agents the way they'd hire staff — except the agents run continuously, instantly, and at a fraction of the cost.

Zero

Automated patient communication system for independent clinics.

An AI agent available on RAnA, purpose-built for independent clinics. Zero connects to a clinic's existing WhatsApp number and handles patient communication automatically: appointment booking, digital intake, queue updates, reminders, and follow-ups — all without adding any staff.
